Eden Rock
http://www.edenrockdive.com/
Eden Rock Diving Center is the gateway to Eden Rock and Devil's Grotto two of Cayman's most loved shore dives. Great for diving and snorkeling, enter the water at one of two shore diving ladders into 3 feet of water and swim out over the gently sloping bottom until the reef rises to within a few feet of the surface before dropping in vertical cliff faces to a 40 sand bottom.
